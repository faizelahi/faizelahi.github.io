About Me

As a junior cyber security analyst, I am eager to contribute my knowledge and skills to help protect organizations from cyber threats. With a strong background in computer science and experience in security risk management, I have a solid foundation in the principles of cyber security. My studies and practical experience have given me a solid understanding of security protocols, network security and incident response.
I am dedicated to staying current with the latest security technologies and trends, and am constantly seeking to expand my knowledge in the field. My analytical skills and attention to detail have helped me to identify and mitigate security risks, and I am able to work well in a team environment to develop and implement security protocols. I am also well-versed in the use of various security tools, including firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and security information and event management solutions.
